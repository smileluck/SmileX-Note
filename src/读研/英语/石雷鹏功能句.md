# 石雷鹏《作文30个功能句》笔记
## 一、小作文高频功能句（英语一&二）
### （一）自我介绍（写给陌生人）
- As ...(身份)who… (进一步介绍), 作为......, 我......
- I am…(身份),who…(进一步介绍) 我是......
- 示例1: As a senior in this university who is particularly fond of reading in this library in my spare time, I…（作为一名业余时间非常喜欢在图书馆看书的本校大四学生,我……）
- 示例2: I am Li Ming, an international student from China who will study in your country.（我是来自中国的留学生李明,我将在贵国学习）

### （二）寒暄（写给熟人）
- I am particularly/quite/very delighted to …（我很高兴......）
- It's so great to …（非常高兴......）
- 示例1: I am very delighted to recall the past two months when I worked as an editor for your magazine.（我很高兴回想起在贵杂志担任编辑的这两个月｡）
- 示例2: It's so great to recall the first successful charity sale held by our class last year.（非常高兴回想起去年我们班成功举办的首次慈善义卖活动｡）
- 可替换表达: 如果不会写delighted, 可以用happy 代替｡
- 注:信件或通知的读者为陌生人时,也可使用寒喧功能句来引出背景信息｡

### （三）（写信/通知）目的
- I am writing to … /I am writing for the purpose of …（我写信来是为了……）
- sth. is/are for the purpose of …（......旨在......）
- 示例1: I am writing for the purpose of making suggestions for improving the service here.（我写信来是想为改进这里(图书馆)的服务提一些建议｡）
- 示例2: This notice is for the purpose of recruiting 25 volunteers.（本通知旨在招募25名志愿者｡）

### （四）建议/要求
- I suggest that …（我建议...…）
- .. is/are(not) supposed to …（...(不)应该......）
- It is (not) advisable (for sb.) to do sth.（(不)建议(某人)做某事）
- It is (not)necessary/appropriate (for sb.) to …（(某人)做某事是(没)有必要/(不)合适的）
- 示例1 : I suggest that you apply for an English teaching position in training organizations.（我建议你申请培训机构的英语教学岗位｡）
- 示例2: You are supposed to prolong the opening hours of reading room for students to study.（你们(图书馆)应该延长阅览室的开放时间,让他们(备考学生)在此学习｡）
- 示例3: It is advisable to purchase more latest books.（建议购入更多最新的图书｡）
- 可替换表达: "It is very necessary…”也可写为“It is of vital necessity”（极重要的"）｡其中vital意为 “必不可少的”｡

### （五）原因(强调句)
- It is … that enables/leads/causes sb. to …（正是.….使得/致使/导致某人......）
- 示例1: It is your devoted work that enables us to enjoy a positive learning atmosphere here.（正是你们尽心尽力的工作使得我们享受到了良好的学习氛围｡）

### （六）积极意义(排比句)
- …will bring sb..., arouse one's enthusiasm/passion for…,and help sb…·（…将带给某人.....,唤起某人对.….的热情,并帮助某人..…·）
- 示例: Practice and diligence will bring you experience and inspiration, arouse your enthusiasm for study, and help you to make progress every day.（实践和勤奋将带给你经验和灵感,唤起你对学习的热情,并帮助你每天进步｡）

### （七）表达感谢或不满
- I am quite/much grateful/obliged (to sb.)for …（我非常感谢.…·）
- I am much/rather disappointed/angry with…(常用于投诉)（我对..….感到非常失望/愤怒｡）
- 示例1: I am much obliged for your hard work.（我想说,非常感谢你们的辛勤工作。）
- 示例2: I am rather disappointed with the quality of your product.（我对你们的产品质量感到非常失望。）

### （八）表达期待
- I am looking forward to ...（我期待......）
- I hope to/that ...（我希望......）
- 示例1: I am looking forward to your favorable reply.（我期待您的肯定答复。）
- 示例 2: I sincerely hope that you can participate in the contest.（我真诚地希望您能参加此次比赛。）

### （九）个人观点(在应用文中也可用于表达期待)
- I believe/deem/am convinced that...（我相信/认为/坚信......）
- 示例: I deem that with these measures taken, students' physical condition will be enhanced.（我认为采取了这些措施,学生的健康状况将得到提升。）

### （十）对比
- Compared with.....较级(或含比较意义的表达..（与......相比,.....更/则.....）
- 示例: Compared with students' demand in study, the number and variety of books are not adequate.（与学生们的学习需求相比,书的数量和种类则不足。）

### （十一）典型特征
- ...be marked/characterized by...（......的典型特征在于......）
- 示例: College life can be characterized by a rich variety of activities.（大学生活的典型特征在于丰富多样的活动。）

### （十二）事件背景
- ...is scheduled to begin/start...(时间.(地点),which will last (for)..(时间段)（..........(时间)在.....(地点)开始,将持续......(时间段)）
- ...is scheduled to be held....(地点)（......将在.....(地点)举办）
- 示例1: This ceremony is scheduled to begin at 9:00 am on June 26th in the Center of Students' Activities, which will last for approximately two hours.（典礼定于6月26日上午9:00在学生活动中心开始,将持续约2小时。）
- 示例 2: The international conference on globalization is scheduled to be held in our university.（有关全球化的国际会议将在我校举办。）
- 可替换表达: ". is scheduled to..."也可写为"... will/is going to..."。

### （十三）值得去做(用于推荐)
- ...is particularly worth doing（......非常值得......）
- 示例: This movie is particularly worth watching for two reasons.（这部电影非常值得一看,有两个原因。）

### （十四）说明自己忙碌
- … I was extremely/exceedingly busy during …(时间段),because/since I focused all my eyesight on …（在.….(时间段) 我非常忙碌,因为我把所有注意力都放在了..….上）
- 示例: As you know, during the past six months, I was exceedingly busy because I focused all my eyesight on the preparation for the national entrance exams for postgraduate studies.（您是知道的,在过去的这半年中,我非常忙碌,因为我把所有注意力放在了研究生入学考试的准备上｡）

## 二、图画作文功能句总结(英语一)
### （一）概述图画
- This is a simple but enlightening picture (which vividly illustrates a common phenomenon among…)（这是一张简单但有启发性的图画(,生动展示了存在千…..之中的一个普遍现象)）
- 可替换表达: 
  - enlightening 也可以写为inspiring､illuminating｡如果图画具有其他特征(比如讽刺性), 可改为对应的表达(如satirical)｡
  - picture 也可以写为cartoon､drawing｡

### （二）图画细节(时间一地点一人/物一行为一伴随)
- …(时间/地点),there be … (人/物),v-ing… (行为),with … (伴随特征)（在…...时间/地点,有…..,正在……,与此同时…..）
- …(地点)verb (动作,如sit) … (人/物),v-ing …(行为),with... (伴随特征)（在….地点(动作,如坐着)……,正在…… ,与此同时…..）
- 示例1: On the path to the top of a high mountain, there is a youngster sitting for rest and sighing,"l am too tired to climb."（在通往高山顶的路上,有一个年轻人正坐着休息,并叹气道:"累了!我不爬了。"）
- 示例2: Beside a table sit four youngsters, staring at their smartphones, with tasty food uneaten.（四个年轻人坐在一张餐桌旁,正盯着自己的智能手机,同时桌子上的美食却丝毫未动。）

### （三）对比反差
- Although...(寻常情况),...(反常之处)（尽管.....,但是......）
- 示例: Although they gather to enhance friendship, scarcely do these guys chat with each other.（尽管他们是为了增进友谊而聚餐,但是这些人彼此间几乎没有交流。）

### （四）文字说明
- Several (或具体字数)Chinese characters can be noticed below the picture, which say(that)...（我们能注意到图画下面有几个汉字,写的.....）
- 示例: Two Chinese characters can also be noticed below the picture, which say "on the journey."（我们还能注意到图画下面有两个汉字,写的是"途中"。）
- 可替换表达: can be noticed也可以写为are given,句子语义变为"图画下面还给出了两个汉字"。

### （五）图画联系主旨
- The picture/drawing/cartoon, apparently, can be associated with ...（显然,这幅图画/漫画能让人联想到.....）
- 示例: This drawing, apparently, can be associated with two distinct attitudes in front of adversities.（显然,这幅图画能让人联想到面对逆境时两种截然不同的态度。）
- 可替换表达: 
  - apparently也可以写为 naturally(多写作can be naturally associated),此时表达的语义是"能让人自然而然联想到"。
  - associated 也可以写为 connected。

### （六）普遍现象
- Similarly/However, in reality, a host of people...(大多数人的行为)（同样/然而,在现实生活里,很多人......）
- 示例: However, in reality, a host of young people tend to give up when encountering challenges during their pursuit of success.（然而,在现实生活里,很多年轻人在追求成功的过程中,往往一遇到挑战便会放弃。）

### （七）(现象/人/物的)典型特征
- ...be marked/characterized by ...（......典型/突出特征在....）
- 示例1: Successful individuals are marked by their various excellent qualities, including diligence, optimism, confidence, a strong will, and perseverance.（成功人士的典型特征在于他们身上的各种优秀品质,包括勤奋、乐观、自信、坚强的意志力以及持之以恒的精神。）
- 示例2: The guy in the left photo is characterized by his face with the name' BECKHAM" on it.（左边照片中的年轻人的突出特征在于他的脸,上面写有BECKHAM的名字。）

### （八）值得关注
- ...is particularly worth concerning ( for the reason that ....（......常值得关注/注意(,因......）
- 示例1: This phenomenon is particularly worth concerning.（这种现象非常值得关注。）
- 示例 2: These two pictures are particularly worth concerning for the reason that they focus on how parents should educate their kids in life and study.（这两幅图很值得注意,因为它们聚焦的是父母在生活和学习方面应如何教育孩子。）

### （九）原因(只关心...,却忽视了.....
- ... focus eyesight only on .., ignoring ...（只关心......,却忽视了......）
- 示例: This trend is particularly worth concerning for the reason that they focus eyesight only on the virtual world, ignoring the real life.（这种趋势很值得关注,因为这些人只关心虚拟世界,却忽视了现实生活。）
- 可替换表达: 
  - focus eyesight only on 可以写为 concentrate only on、 only emphasize、 pay attention only to.
  - ignoring 也可以写为 overlooking、neglecting。

### （十）关键原因/重大意义(强调句)
- It is ... that leads/enables/causes sb. to ...（正是....导致/使得/致使某人......）
- 示例: It is their pessimistic attitude that causes them to see no hope, no chance, or no possibility of change.（正是他们的悲观心态导致他们看不到希望,看不到机会,也看不到改变的可能。）

### （十一）积极意义(排比句)
- ... will bring sb. ..., arouse one's enthusiasm/passion for ..., and help sb. ...（....带给某人......,唤起某人.....热情,并帮助.....）
- 示例: I am firmly convinced that perseverance always brings me power, arouses my enthusiasm for the future, and helps me to conquer so many hardships.（我坚信持之以恒的精神总能带给我力量,唤起我对未来的热情,并帮助我克服许多困难。）

### （十二）危害
- ... may constitute an obstacle that hinders...(成长、进步、发展等)（.....可能会构成一种障碍,阻碍....）
- 示例: As a matter of fact, the thought of "giving up" may constitute an obstacle that hinders many people's growth and progress in work, life, and study.（事实上,"放弃"这种想法可能会构成一种障碍,阻碍很多人在工作、生活和学习中取得成长和进步。）

### （十三）个人观点
- (As a ...) I believe/deem/am convinced that..（(作为.....,)我相信/认为/坚信......）
- 示例: As a senior who is preparing for the national entrance exams for postgraduate studies, I am firmly convinced that perseverance always brings me power, arouses my enthusiasm for the future, and helps me to conquer so many hardships.（作为一名正在准备研究生入学考试的大四学生,我坚信持之以恒的精神总能带给我力量,唤起我对未来的热情,并帮助我克服许多困难。）

### （十四）观点+比喻
- To my understanding, ... can be likened to/seen as ...（在我看来,....可以比作/看作.......）
- 示例1: To my understanding, persistence can be likened to a road leading to success.（在我看来,坚持可以比作一条通往成功的道路。）
- 示例 2: To my understanding, confidence can be seen as a key which unlocks the gate to a promising future.（在我看来,自信可以看作一把钥匙,打开通往光明未来的大门。）

### （十五）建议(某人应该/不应该做某事)
- It is (not) advisable for sb. to do sth.（某人应该/不应该做某事）
- 示例1: Accordingly, it is advisable for us to utilize modern technological products rationally.（因此,我们应该理性使用现代科技产品。）
- 示例 2: I am convinced that it is not advisable for youngsters to be slaves of mobile phones.（我坚信年轻人不应该成为手机的奴隶。）
- 可替换表达: 这一功能句也可以写为"sb.be (not) supposed to do sth.",或者简单写为"sb. should (not) do sth."

### （十六）号召
- Everyone should bear in mind that...(应牢记)（每个人都应牢记..）
- Society as a whole should foster a positive atmosphere to ...(应营造积极的氛围)（整个社会应营造一种积极的氛围,以.....）
- 示例1: Thus, everyone should bear in mind that "Never give up. You will eventually achieve your dreams."（因此,每个人都应牢记"永不放弃,你的梦想终会实现"。）
- 示例2: Society as a whole should foster a positive atmosphere to advocate public virtues.（整个社会应营造一种积极的氛围,以倡导公共美德。）

### （十七）举例/列举(在其他功能后详述)
- A case in point is …（一个典型的例子就是……）
- …, including/such as …（......,包括/比如......）
- 示例1: A case in point is myself.（一个典型的例子就是我自己）
- 示例2 : We should utilize modern technological products rationally, including the Internet, cellphones, TV and computers.（我们应理性使用现代科技产品,包括网络､手机､电视以及电脑｡）

## 三、图表作文功能句总结(英语二)
### （一）概述图表
- Given/This is a column chart that clearly illustrates the dramatic changes in… (根据图表主题填写),from…(时间)to…(时间).（所给的/这是一个柱状图,清晰展示了XX年到XX年……的显著变化｡）
- 示例: This is a column chart that clearly illustrates the dramatic changes in shares of domestic, American, and Japanese car brands in the Chinese market from 2008 to 2009.（这是一个柱状图,清晰展示了2008 年到2009 年中､美､日系汽车品牌在中国汽车市场份额的明显变化｡）
- 可替换表达: 
  - column chart 的意思是柱状图,还可以写为bar chart｡此外常考的图表还有:饼状图pie chart､曲线图line graph ､表格table｡
  - dramatic changes 也可以写为significant changes｡

### （二）数据上升/下降/稳定(适用于动态变化图表)
- 【数据上升/下降】…(时间段),the share of… ascended/descended remarkably, reaching approximately…(数据)/from its peak at…(数据)to…(数据).（在.….期间,……的份额明显上升/下降达到约…… /从其最高值.… .降到……）
- 【数据上升/下降】… displayed a sharp increase/decline, from ..(数据)to..(数据).（……表现出明显的上升/下降从.… .到……）
- 【数据稳定】The number/percentage of… maintained/remained stable at…(数据).（……的数据/占比稳定在……）
- 示例: During the period, the share of domestic brands ascended remarkably, reaching approximately 33%. By contrast, sales of Japanese brands displayed a sharp decline, from 35% to 26%. Meanwhile, the percentage of American brands remained stable at 12% .（在此期间,中国品牌的份额明显上升,达到约33%｡相比之下,日系品牌的销量呈急剧下降趋势,从35%降到26%｡同时,美系品牌的占比稳定在12%）
- 可替换表达: 
  - ascended/descended remarkably 可替换为increased/decreased remarkably｡
  - displayed 可替换为showed｡
  - maintained/remained stable at 可替换为maintained/remained the same level at …

### （三）占比排序(适用于静态图表)
- 【占比最大】Overall,… accounts for the largest proportion (of… ) ,at...(数据).（总的来说,…… 占最大比重,占……）
- 【占比其次】The next are… and… ,with…(数据)and…(数据)respectively.（接下来是....和......,分别占....和......）
- 【占比最小/其他】Finally come(s)… ,at…(数据).（排在最后的是…… ,占…..）
- 示例1: Overall, seniors have the largest proportion of part-time job takers, at 88.24%. The next are juniors and sophomores, with 71.93% and 71.13% respectively. Finally come freshmen, at 67.77%.（总体上,大四学生兼职比例最高,达到了 88.24%;紧随其后的是大三大二学生,分别是71.93% 和71.13%;排在最后的是大一学生,占67.77%。）
- 示例2: Overall, the expense on New Year gifts is the largest proportion, accounting for 40%. The next is the expenditure on transportation and dining, with the same 20%. Finally comes the total spending on others, at 20%.（总体上,新年礼物的花销占最大比重,达到40%。其次是交通和聚餐费用,均占20%。最后是其他花销的总和,为20%。）

### （四）图表联系主旨
- Such statistics can be naturally associated with ...（这样的数据自然让人联想到......）
- 示例: Such statistics can be associated with the fierce competition in China's car market.（这样的数据能让人联想到中国汽车市场的激烈竞争。）

### （五）引出原因(通常用在图表联系主旨后面)
- But what resulted in the differences above? To my understanding, at least two reasons can account for this phenomenon.（但什么导致了以上差异?在我看来,至少有两个原因可以解释这种现象。）

### （六）典型特征+结果(原因1)
- For one thing, ... be characterized by .., which enables ... to ...（一方面,.....典型特征在.....,这使得....）
- 示例: For one thing, in recent years, developing countries were characterized by their economic boom, which enabled residents there to possess additional money to purchase mobile phones.（一方面,近年来,发展中国家的典型特征在于其经济的蓬勃发展,这使得当地居民有额外的钱来购买手机｡）

### （七）对比+结果(原因2)
- For another, compared with ….,…比较级(或含比较意义的表达)…,so…（另一方面,与…..相比,……更……,因此....）
- 示例: For another, compared with before, the Sino-Japanese relations got worse, so a larger proportion of residents in China did not purchase Japanese products, including cars.（另一方面,与过去相比,中日关系变得更糟糕,因此更多中国居民不再购买包括汽车在内的日本产品｡）

### （八）建议1 (建议关注或建议做某事)
- (As a result ) it is essential/necessary for sb. to (focus more eyesight on)….,(due to ...).（(因此,)……有必要(更关注)……,(因为……)）
- (In my understanding,)… is/are supposed to (pay more attention to)…（(在我看来,)……应该(更加关注)……）
- 示例: As a result, Chinese manufacturers are supposed to pay more attention to the domestic market, due to its great potential.（因此,中国汽车制造商应该更关注国内市场｡因为其潜力巨大｡）

### （九）建议2(建议制定综合策略)
- Meanwhile, it is essential/advisable for sb. to work out comprehensive strategies to...（同时,某人有必要/应当制定综合策略......）
- 示例: Meanwhile, it is essential for them to work out comprehensive strategies to enhance the quality of cars and improve after-sales service.（同时,他们有必要制定综合策略来提升汽车的质量和售后服务。）

### （十）积极意义(排比句)
- ... will bring sb. .., arouse one's enthusiasm/passion for .., and help sb. ....（....带给某人...., 唤起某人....热情,并帮助某人.....）
- 示例: Studying abroad brings young people more opportunities, arouses their enthusiasm for foreign cultures, and helps them broaden their horizons.（出国留学带给年轻人更多机会,唤起他们对外国文化的热情,并帮助他们开阔眼界。）

### （十一）展望未来(通常放在建议后)
- If they do their utmost, the future of .. will be hopeful/promising.（如果他们竭尽全力,......的明天将一片光明。）
- 示例: If they do their utmost, the future of the mobile phone industry will be hopeful.（如果他们竭尽全力,手机产业的明天将一片光明。）

### （十二）个人观点(可与建议和排比连用,如下面示例)
- (As a ...) I believe/deem/am convinced that ...（(作为......)我相信/认为/坚信.....）
- (As a ...,) I prefer doing/to do ...（(作为......)我喜欢.......）
- 示例: As an adult student, I deem that it is necessary for us to do part-time jobs, which may bring us an opportunity to know the society, arouse our passion for life, and help ease our financial burden.（作为一个成年学生,我认为我们有必要做兼职,这会带给我们了解社会的契机,唤起我们对生活的热爱,并有助于减轻我们的经济负担。）

# 英语模板
