# 考研英语二作文高频易错词+万能句型终极清单
## 一、 高频易错词（按场景分类，考前反复默写）
### （一） 通用基础易错词
| 错误写法 | 正确写法 | 备注 |
|----------|----------|------|
| shard / sharing bike | shared bike | 共享单车（固定表达） |
| signficant | significant | 重要的（大作文高频词） |
| relevent | relevant | 相关的（如relevant authorities） |
| damand | demand | 需求（固定搭配demand for） |
| differents | differences | 差异（名词复数） |
| furture | future | 未来 |
| shoud | should | 情态动词 |
| natrually | naturally | 自然地 |
| charaterized | characterized | 以……为特征（be characterized by） |
| persepective | perspective | 观点（from my perspective） |

### （二） 单复数/词性易错点
1.  **可数名词复数**：reason→reasons；purpose→purposes；bicycle→bicycles；mobile phone→mobile phones
2.  **形容词vs副词**：simple（形）→修饰名词；simply（副）→修饰动词；easy（形）→easily（副）
3.  **名词vs形容词**：technology（名）→technological（形，如technological development）；scholar（名）→academic（形，如academic activities）

### （三） 固定搭配易错介词
| 错误搭配 | 正确搭配 | 例句 |
|----------|----------|------|
| apply this program | apply **for** this program | 申请项目 |
| participate activities | participate **in** activities | 参与活动 |
| demand of | demand **for** | 对……的需求 |
| need of | need **for** | 对……的需求 |
| at 2022 | in 2022 | 年份前用in |
| in the campus | on campus | 在校园里（不加the） |

## 二、 万能句型（小作文+大作文通用，直接套用）
### （一） 小作文万能句型（六大场景全覆盖）
#### 1.  道歉信
- 开篇：I am writing to apologize to you because I [做错的事，如lost your English novel].
- 表达歉意：I feel truly sorry for my carelessness, as I know that [对方的损失].
- 解决方案：To make up for my mistake, I will [具体措施，如buy you an identical new copy].

#### 2.  邀请信
- 开篇：I am writing to invite you to [参与的活动] as [身份，如a judge].
- 活动信息：The event is scheduled to be held [时间] on campus.
- 结尾恳请：We would greatly appreciate it if you could accept our invitation.

#### 3.  申请信
- 开篇：I am writing to formally apply for [项目名称，如the overseas short-term study program].
- 优势描述：I have rich experience in [能力，如communicating with foreigners].
- 结尾：I would greatly appreciate it if you could consider my application.

#### 4.  感谢信
- 开篇：I am writing to express my sincere gratitude to you for [对方的帮助].
- 收获描述：I have gained a lot from [活动，如this program].
- 结尾：I am truly grateful to you for your support throughout this program.

### （二） 大作文万能句型（三大图表通用三段式）
#### 1.  第一段：描述图表（柱状图/折线图/饼图）
| 图表类型 | 开篇点题 | 数据描述 | 趋势总结 |
|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| 柱状图 | This bar chart illustrates the changes in [主题，如online food delivery orders] from [年份区间]. | Starting at [起点数据], the number rose steadily to [终点数据]. | Overall, there was a dramatic upward trend during this period. |
| 折线图 | This line chart clearly shows the changes in the usage of [主题，如shared bikes] from [年份区间]. | During this period, the usage rose remarkably, reaching approximately [数据]. | It is evident that the demand for [主题] has grown substantially. |
| 饼图 | This pie chart illustrates the distribution of [主题，如purposes for using shared bikes] in [年份]. | Commuting accounts for the largest proportion, at [百分比], while [其他类别] make up [百分比]. | Such statistics are naturally associated with people’s lifestyles. |

#### 2.  第二段：分析原因（万能2个角度）
- 总起句：Several factors can account for this significant trend / phenomenon.
- 角度1（科技发展）：Advanced technology is characterized by high efficiency, which has [带来的影响，如made online food delivery a reality].
- 角度2（生活方式）：With the accelerating pace of modern life, an increasing number of people tend to [行为，如choose shared bikes for short-distance travel].

#### 3.  第三段：总结趋势/建议
- 建议句：Relevant authorities should pay attention to the [行业名称] industry, given its great potential and the need for standardized supervision.
- 趋势预测：If they make great efforts, the future of the [行业名称] industry will be promising.
- 升华句：This phenomenon reflects the continuous improvement of people’s living standards and the transformation of consumption patterns in China.

## 三、 写作避坑终极提醒
1.  **标点**：全程用英文标点（. ,），杜绝中文句号（。）；状语短语后加逗号（如To make up for my mistake, ...）。
2.  **时态**：描述过去数据用一般过去时（rose/reached）；分析原因用现在时；预测未来用will/be likely to。
3.  **字数**：小作文100词左右，大作文150词左右，避免过少或过多。
4.  **语气**：正式书信（对外教/负责人）不用口语化表达（如用appreciate替代like）。
